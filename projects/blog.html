Blog Website

  • Tech Stack: PHP, HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL, Blade, Laravel

Offers an extensive set of features for bloggers. It includes categories, tags, comments, and email notifications, allowing you to create and organize your blog posts effectively.

The website facilitates user engagement by enabling comments and email notifications. Visitors can actively participate in discussions by leaving comments on your blog posts. Additionally, the email notification system ensures that users receive updates on new blog posts and comments.

The website provides flexibility and scalability. You have the freedom to customize the design and layout to suit your preferences. With the robust backend powered by Laravel and SQL, the website can handle increasing traffic and content as your blog grows.

It is a responsive website that works well on all devices. It is also optimized for search engines, making it easier for your target audience to find your blog.
