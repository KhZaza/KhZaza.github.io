Linkedin Job Bot

  • Tech Stack: Python, HTML, CSS, Selenium

Developed a LinkedIn Job Bot that automates the job application process, saving time and effort for users by logging into their LinkedIn account and applying to relevant job postings based on provided keywords.

Integrated the user's resume and personal information into the bot, enabling the generation of personalized cover letters or application forms for each job application. Showcased the ability to tailor the bot's output to individual user preferences and requirements.

Demonstrated expertise in web automation, data extraction, and third-party platform integration by building a bot that interacts with LinkedIn. Utilized web scraping techniques, browser automation, and data manipulation to create a seamless and functional solution.
